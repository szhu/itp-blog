Fabrication Week 4
Enclosure with controls

For my thesis, I want to create an input device that I can hold in one hand. For this assignment, I decided to make a prototype of a small person of that.

I bought some push buttons to mount onto a cardboard box.

I wanted to position that buttons so that when I held the box, each of my fingers would be over a button.

The buttons turned out to be extremely easy to mount; I just need to punch a hole in the box, stick the button through, and tighten it with the included nut.
